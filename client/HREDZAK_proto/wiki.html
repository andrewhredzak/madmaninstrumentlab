Seeded Wiki Notes

Hredzak Family Archive

Category: Repository

This prototype archive is intended to centralize historical photos, parish material, family recordings, and future video uploads into one browsable collection.

Krcma

Category: Place

Several assets in the gallery reference Krcma directly. This page should eventually note the exact location, dates represented in the photos, and who identified the scenes.

Old Country Photos

Category: Image Collection

The Old Country images appear to be heritage references that should be tagged by place, era, original source, and whether the image is a duplicate or restoration.

Parish History Document

Category: Document

The parish history PDF is a key text record in the current prototype and should be cross-linked to people, locations, and any images that reference the same church or surrounding area.
